# Schneiders Research Agent

This repository tracks ongoing research topics for the Schneiders team. Each issue represents a research area we monitor on a regular cadence.

Research is conducted automatically by our AI agent and findings are published to the [team wiki](https://wiki.bondbuilt.ai) under the Research section.

## How It Works

- **Open issues** = active research topics
- **Labels** indicate frequency: `weekly`, `monthly`
- **Labels** indicate domain: `ai`, `ecommerce`, `equestrian`, `robotics`, `farm-operations`
- Each run posts a summary as an issue comment and publishes a full report to the wiki
- Recurring topics stay open; one-shot research gets closed when complete

## Research Domains

| Domain | What We Watch |
|---|---|
| AI -- General | Emerging AI capabilities, agent frameworks, tools relevant to how we operate |
| E-commerce & Retail | Shopify ecosystem, fulfillment innovation, customer experience, DTC trends |
| Equestrian Market | Consumer trends, competitor moves, new products, brand positioning |
| Robotics & Automation | Warehouse robotics, fulfillment automation, last-mile delivery |
| Farm & Barn Operations | Operator pain points, group buying, advisory services, software |

## Adding a Research Topic

Open a new issue with:
- Clear title describing what to research
- Label for frequency (`weekly` or `monthly`)
- Label for domain
- Body describing what specifically to look for and why it matters to Schneiders

## Output

Reports are published to `wiki.bondbuilt.ai/research/` and linked from each issue comment after each run.
